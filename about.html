Run with AMH

AMH Catalyst Center was built by women who understand what it takes to raise capital.

After decades working across startups, venture-backed companies, and large organizations, Jenny and Grace led, designed, and executed a $420M small business fund that supported 336,000 founders — 53% of them women — and unlocked 7x leverage to keep underserved businesses alive and local economies moving.

Through that work, they saw the same dynamic again and again:

Talented women building real businesses, yet navigating a capital system driven by insider access and narrow pattern matching.

Instead of asking women founders to simply work harder within a system that wasn't built for them, Jenny and Grace built something new: a platform that prepares women entrepreneurs to compete, succeed, and change the system.

Today, under Jenny's leadership, AMH gives founders what they need before they walk into the room: pitch-ready business models, airtight financials, compelling narratives, and the confidence that comes from real preparation. Through proprietary AI-powered tools, expert-led guidance, and trusted capital networks, AMH helps women founders move from idea to investor-ready with clarity and momentum.

The name honors their daughters Angie, Maddie, and Hannah (AMH) and the future it represents: a world where women founders can access the capital they need to build, and capital systems evolve to reflect their talent and potential. Because when women founders are equipped to compete at the highest levels, the future of venture changes for everyone.
